**Target:** R(5,5) n=25, leaderboard capacity 500
**Logs:** `logs/experiment-20260315-213310/`

## Config

- tree2: beam=100, depth=10, bias=0.5 (1 worker)
- Both: --init leaderboard, release mode

## Analysis
- tree2 is **11x faster** per round and gets **4.6x more admissions**
- The incremental delta approach is the key win
- tree fully plateaued (0 admissions in last 37 rounds)
- tree2 approaching plateau (51 admissions in last 210 rounds)

## Decision
tree2 becomes the production default strategy. tree demoted to reference/ablation.
